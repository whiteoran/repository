INSPIRE INITIATE INNOVATE

We at SCRO focus on building autonomous multi- rotor copters and fixed-wing aerial vehicles. The students here work across various domains like Design and Fabrication, Electronics and Control Systems, Image Processing and Corporate to come up with sustainable solutions to real world problems.

  • Image Processing

    Image Processing means processing digital image by means of a digital computer. In our domain, we get raw images as input from the drones and process them to get the desired output.

  • Corporate

    SCRO Corporate works on various business fields such as Sponsorship and Partnerships, Marketing, Event Management, Creatives, etc.. to enhance the operations of the Organization

  • Electronics

    The motors and propellers are the drone technology, which move the UAV into the air and to fly in any direction or hover. Electronics domain work to design and develop flight and electronic systems as per requirement.

  • Design and Fabrication

    Design and Fabrication is responsible for the Structural and propulsion aspect of the drone and also its Fabrication. Our job is to make the drone light and sturdy.
