WHAT MOTIVATES US



  • We at SCRO(Student Copter Research Organization) focus on building autonomous multi- rotor copters and fixed-wing aerial vehicles. The students here work across various domains like Design and Fabrication, Electronics and Control Systems, Image Processing (Computer Vision) and Corporate to come up with sustainable solutions to real world problems.

  • SCRO is one of the highest funded UAV-related research-oriented teams in India continuously developing and suiting present needs. The team competes in various international competitions including IMAV and AUVSI-SUAS. We take pride in winning various national level projects and conducting air shows in various universities. These achievements have gradually resulted in many government and private organizations crediting SCRO for their work and assigning projects for practical use in various capacities. The team is positive to continue their work with the same coherent aims and objectives and continue doing developments in this field to the best of our capacity.
